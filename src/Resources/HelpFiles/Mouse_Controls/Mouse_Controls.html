Mouse Controls

Several basic surface and volume viewing and navigation functions are controlled with the mouse.
  • Orientation: Moving the mouse in any direction, within the viewing window, with the Left mouse button pressed, changes the orientation of any 3D surface being viewed.
  • Zoom: Moving the mouse up or down with the Left mouse button + Command key (Mac) or Control key (PC, Linux) pressed, will zoom surface or volume display.  This can also be done with just scrolling the mouse wheel.
  • Pan: Moving the mouse in any direction, with the Left mouse button + Shift key pressed will move the surface or volume display to a different part of the Viewing window.
  • ID: Clicking on the brain surface, will identify a brainordinate with a sphere marking the location.  The Information Box will also appear, displaying information on the particular vertex that was clicked.
    • Delete the ID sphere by clicking it again.
